How To Play

  • Pairs of gems, called Active Gems appear at the top of your board and descend towards the bottom.

  • You can move and rotate Active Gems to anywhere you'd like, using the arrow keys, or your mouse, touchscreen or gamepad.

  • Try to set gems next to gems of the same color in any direction, forming Runs. Some gems that drop are round. These are Crash Gems.

  • The goal is to combine Runs of like-colored gems with Crash Gems of the same color.

  • When you crash Runs of gems, you get points, and drop Timer Gems on your targeted opponent.

  • Every time you drop your Active Gems, the number on Timer Gems decreases, until they become normal gems.

  • If you're facing more than one opponent, you can target any one of them using the number keys or by clicking or tapping their board.

  • If gems fill your board, you lose. If you (or your teammate) are the last player standing, you win!
